"Set the overall preset of graphics quality." Sets every option to the selected value at once. The overall color and atmospherics of the scene are hardly changed, along with the model quality and bloom, but textures are very blurry, lighting and shadowing are inaccurate, and the grass is less dense. IMPORTANT: this is tied directly to the quality of light shafts. Turning this to medium or lower and every other option up to very high will result in about a 10 FPS increase at the cost of lower resolution light shafts.
